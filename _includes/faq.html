Frequently Asked Questions

Thanks for asking!

How do I contact you?

Email is the best way is to communicate with us, we read them all! Our email address is on the footer of every page. Just scroll down and look for the envelope. Or simply click here.

How mature is Couverture?

Couverture is currently in beta. If you see problems let us know by email, you will be thanked warmly.

Does it work with non-GitHub providers?

Not right now. If you are interested to use Couverture with other providers such as Bitbucket or GitLab, just let us know which by email as it will help priotarize what to focus on next.

Can I use Couverture with my private repos?

Not yet, but this will likely be a paid feature eventually. Interested? Let us know by email. The higher the demand, the faster it will happen.

What does "couverture" mean?

It's a French word and it means "blanket" in English. Random factoid: Couverture is also a form of chocolate with a high proportion of cocoa butter, used in dipping and coating to make things even more delicious.
